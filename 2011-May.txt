In this alpha release the code for handling bookmarks and homes shares has 
been rewritten, the IP address scanner, the print and search classes, the 
mounter and the previewer have been converted to KCompositeJobs and they use 
KJobs to do the work. Most of the dialogs have become private core helper 
classes. Bugs have been fixed.
