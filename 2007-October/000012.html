The Smb4K team is proud to announce the immediate availability of version 
0.8.6 of the advanced network neighborhood browser for KDE. This maintenance 
release fixes a crash that several people experienced when mounting shares 
that contained special characters.

ChangeLog Smb4K 0.8.6:
- Fixed a crash in Smb4KMounter::findShareByPath() (closes #11499, #11543 and
  #11973).

As always, the source can be downloaded from our project page at 
http://developer.berlios.de/projects/smb4k/ .
